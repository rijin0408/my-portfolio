About Me

       I am Bhenz Mharl L. Bartolome, a 22-year-old BSIT graduate from Bulacan State University. I am now in my 4th month as an ICT teacher at Montessori de Sagrada Familia, where I guide students in exploring technology and developing real-world skills.

       I enjoy learning new tools, software, and teaching strategies and finding ways to use them in my classes. I share what I learn through practical projects and lessons that help students think creatively and work together. My goal is to guide students in building confidence, improving their problem-solving skills, and using technology in meaningful ways.
